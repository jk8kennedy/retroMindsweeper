Any game progress or settings are stored locally on your device only. Nothing is transmitted to external servers.

This app does not use analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking systems.

The game does not integrate with third-party data collection services. It is hosted via GitHub Pages, which operates under its own infrastructure policies.

An internet connection is only required to load the game. No user data is sent or received.

This app does not knowingly collect any data from users of any age, including children under 13.
